Ukwa

Description

Ukwa which is known to some as Breadfruit is a Nigerian delicacy that offers a range of nutritional values.

Ukwa can be cooked on its own with no flavour or seasoning because of its natural flavour and delicious nature.

Ingredients

  • fresh ukwa
  • dry fish
  • stock fish
  • grounded fresh pepper
  • palm oil
  • water
  • seasoning cube
  • squeezed fresh bitterleaf

Steps

  1. wash ukwa thoroughly to remove stones and sand in it
  2. put it in a large pot and pour water enough to cover it
  3. set on fire and boil for 30-40 minutes until it is soft. (Continue boiling until it is soft)
  4. remove all bones from the stockfish and dry fish and wash with hot water; add them together with salt to the pot while ukwa is still boiling.
  5. top up water to the ukwa if it has not softened enough
  6. if the ukwa is soft, stop cooking; sieve out the liquid and keep; add red oil, pepper, squeezed bitter leaves; and stir until palm oil changes from red to yellow colour
  7. put it on fire again and stir; allow it to simmer for 10-15 minutes; and serve
