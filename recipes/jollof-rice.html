Jollof Rice

Description

The popular Nigerian jollof rice is a delicacy worth adding to your diet. Its reputation precedes it as it is served in almost all occasions hosted in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Sliced onions
  • Salt
  • Grounded fresh pepper
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, corn, peas)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Seasoning cube
  • Powdered seasoning (curry, thyme, ginger, garlic)
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Season the meat with salt, seasoning cube and sliced onions; Heat up vegetable oil and fry the meat and keep.
  2. Use the vegetable oil in the pot; add sliced onions; add tomato paste after a couple of minutes; Cook until browned; add water and allow it to simmer
  3. Pre-boil your rice in another pot
  4. Add the pre-boiled to the simmering tomato sauce on fire
  5. Add water; Add seasoning to it and stir
  6. Allow water to dry up; Add the mixed vegetables when it is about drying up and stir until done
